About

AutoSureEstimator — independent tools.

We build privacy-first, educational estimators that help US drivers understand typical insurance cost ranges — without sales pressure, and without collecting personal data.

What we are

AutoSureEstimator is an independent educational project focused on insurance pricing transparency. Our tools are designed to be used anonymously — no phone, email, VIN, or sign-up required.

Independent

We are not affiliated with any insurance carrier or agency. Content is designed to help you make better decisions.

Privacy-first

Calculations run in your browser. We don’t request sensitive personal identifiers to use the tools.

Educational ranges

We provide realistic high/low ranges based on aggregated market data — not binding quotes.

Simple methodology

Clear baselines + transparent multipliers. Easy to understand and continuously improved over time.

Important: We do not sell leads, we do not broker policies, and we do not provide legal advice. Always confirm SR-22/FR-44 rules and final pricing with licensed professionals and your state’s DMV.

What we are not

  • Not an insurer: we don’t underwrite policies or handle claims.
  • Not a broker/agent: we don’t place coverage or negotiate rates.
  • Not official quotes: your exact price depends on ZIP, VIN, mileage, carrier rules, and underwriting.
  • Not DMV guidance: requirements can change by state and situation.
Why ranges? Two drivers with similar profiles can still get different prices from different carriers. Showing a range is more honest than a single “magic number”.

Methodology & standards

Insurance pricing is proprietary by carrier, but many baseline signals are well understood at a market level. Our estimators are built from aggregated public/market data and simplified modeling:

  • Baselines: national or state-level average premiums used as a starting point.
  • Multipliers: applied for known high-impact variables (e.g., SR-22 reason, coverage level, age band, state risk).
  • Ranges: we display a low/high band to reflect typical carrier spread and underwriting differences.
Accuracy note: Our outputs are educational estimates. Actual pricing varies significantly by ZIP code, VIN, mileage, credit tier, and carrier underwriting rules. If you see an inconsistency, contact us — we improve continuously.
