Report a Correction

Our data is based on continuously changing state regulations and market averages. If you notice a specific error—such as an outdated minimum coverage requirement for a specific state or a broken calculator function—please let us know.

To help us fix it faster, please include:

  • The URL of the page containing the error.
  • The specific value or text that is incorrect.
  • A source (like a DMV link) for the correct information, if available.
