Privacy Policy.

This policy explains what we collect, what we don’t, and how advertising cookies may be used on AutoSureEstimator.

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Key points:

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • No account or sign-up is required to use our tools.
  • Calculator inputs are intended to be processed locally in your browser to generate estimates (we do not build a database of your form entries).

1) Information we collect

A. Information you provide (calculator inputs)

When you use our estimators, you may enter non-identifying data points (for example: state, age range, violation category, coverage level). We do not ask for your name, physical address, Social Security number, driver’s license number, full policy documents, or VIN.

B. Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and security systems may log basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This is used for security, abuse prevention, and high-level analytics.

2) How we use information

We use information to:

  • Operate and maintain site functionality
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the tools
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

We do not create profiles for the purpose of selling leads to insurance agents or lead aggregators.

3) Cookies & advertising

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site functionality and advertising.

Third-party advertising (Google AdSense)

We may use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and/or other websites. This can include personalized advertising where permitted by applicable law and your settings.

Your choices

  • Manage personalized ads via Google’s ad settings: Google Ads Settings
  • Learn more / opt out of some interest-based ads: aboutads.info
  • You can also control cookies through your browser settings.
Note: If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, cookie consent requirements may apply depending on how you configure advertising/analytics. If you later add a consent banner, this policy remains compatible.

4) Data retention

Calculator data

We do not intend to store your calculator form inputs in a user-identifiable database. The estimator results are generated at the time of use.

Log data

Technical logs (including IP addresses) may be retained for a limited period for security and auditing, then deleted or anonymized. Retention length depends on hosting/security providers and operational needs.

5) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the site. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6) Children’s privacy

AutoSureEstimator is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page when changes are made.
