Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

Summary of Key Points

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • No account sign-up is required to use our tools.
  • Calculator inputs (like age or zip code) are processed locally in your browser to generate estimates and are not permanently stored on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Voluntarily Provide

When you use our cost estimators, you input non-identifying data points such as your age range, state of residence, driving history summary, and vehicle type. We do not ask for, nor do we collect, your name, physical address, email address, phone number, social security number, or Vehicle Identification Number (VIN).

B. Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, when you visit AutoSureEstimator, our web hosting servers automatically log standard technical information. This may include:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Pages visited

This data is used for server health monitoring, security, and aggregate analytics to understand traffic patterns.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of our insurance cost calculators.
  • Analyze website usage trends to improve user experience.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security threats.

We do not create user profiles for the purpose of selling data to third-party insurance agents or lead aggregators.

3. Cookies & Advertising

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information.

Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) to provide interest-based advertisements about goods and services that may be of interest to you.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Your Choices Regarding Ads

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Data Retention

Calculator Data: The specific inputs you enter into our forms are processed transiently (in the moment) to generate your result. We do not retain databases of user calculator inputs.

Log Data: Server logs containing IP addresses are retained for a standard duration necessary for security and auditing purposes, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

5. Your Choices

Since we do not collect personal contact information or require accounts, there is no need to "unsubscribe" from email lists or delete an account. You can control cookies through your browser settings or by using the opt-out links provided in the Advertising section above.
