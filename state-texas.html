Texas Car Insurance: Minimum Requirements & Typical Costs

Use this page as a fast TX reference: legal minimum limits, typical monthly premiums, and next steps to estimate your own rate using our calculator.

Texas minimum requirements (state minimum)

Commonly expressed as 30/60/25:

  • $30,000 bodily injury liability per person
  • $60,000 bodily injury liability per accident
  • $25,000 property damage liability per accident
Tip: Minimum coverage is legal compliance, not full financial protection. Many drivers choose higher limits.

Average monthly cost in Texas (reference)

Liability-only: $119/mo
Full coverage: $210/mo

These are statewide averages used as a baseline. Your final price changes with ZIP code, driving record, vehicle, credit tier (where allowed), and coverage choices.
