Frequently Asked Questions

Are these estimates official insurance quotes?

No. AutoSureEstimator provides educational cost ranges based on aggregated market data and common risk factors. We are not an insurance carrier, broker, or agent. For a binding quote, you must contact a licensed insurer or agent.

Do I need to enter my personal information?

No. Our tools are designed to be anonymous. We do not require your name, phone number, email address, VIN, or Social Security number to generate an estimate.

Why is the estimated range so wide?

Insurance pricing varies by many factors including ZIP code, driving history, vehicle trim, coverage limits, deductibles, credit-based insurance score (where permitted), and carrier underwriting. We show a range to reflect these real-world differences without collecting private data.

Are these tools free to use?

Yes. All calculators on AutoSureEstimator are free to use for informational purposes.

Do you sell insurance or sell my data?

No. AutoSureEstimator does not sell insurance products and does not sell personal data. The site is built to help you understand typical price ranges before you shop.
